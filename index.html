About Hope Is Now

Hope Is Now is dedicated to helping the community by providing school supplies, financial assistance, and essential resources to families in need. We believe every child deserves the tools to succeed in school and every family deserves support during difficult times. Through the generosity of our donors, we extend a hand to the unfortunate, offering relief, encouragement, and opportunities for a brighter future. Our mission is to bring hope, reduce hardship, and build stronger communities together.
